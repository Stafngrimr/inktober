Day Twenty-Six, Connect

Go Back

He couldn't believe what he was seeing.

A few minutes ago, he was just walking down the street and and just happened to turn to glance at and into the window of a house. He saw an arm moving slowly and rather oddly towards a computer and then the fingers detached and cables came out, gliding across the air. It stopped him dead in his tracks and he didn't move for a moment, as he watched the cables extend from the hand and slither towards the pc before hooking themselves into it.

He regained some control of his legs and slowly moved towards the window, noticing that actually at the other end of the arm, there was no upper arm and no torso. There was in fact, no human parts at all attached, and in their place some plastic tubing that lead down to below the bottom of the window, out of his sight. He moved forwards slowly some more, before it was revealed that a small mouse on a tiny laptop, was hooked into this contraption and appeared to be hacking into computer. This was equally, the most ridiculous, unbelievable and strangely adorable thing he'd ever seen. Without realising it he had started to press his face against the glass of the window to get the best possible view, and this cause the mouse to turn around. He looked at the mouse, completely still and wide eyed...
