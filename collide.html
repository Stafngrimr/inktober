Day Seventeen, Collide

Go Back

Experiment-C was a mistake that happened after a late night conversation surrounding the mythical creatures of old. Myself and Doctors Carlton and Edwards would often end days with a brandy and rousing discussion of some description. Though this night what came to mind were the harpy, the gryphon, the dreaded manticore.. what wonderful imagination these myths sparked and at first just for some fun.

Though the discussion turned serious and technical as the brandy flowed and the hours drifted away. How would it even be possible? It seemed so far fetched and ridiculous at first, but the more discussion was had the more answers we'd discover to questions that no longer seemed insurmountable.

It was the very next day that we sat to discuss further, in the sobering light of day to discover that not only did our answers still work, but the number of unanswered questions dwindled to almost nothing. I wish we hadn't felt so inspired by this and stopped to think of the consequences. Now experiment-C has escpaed, experiment-E is in the ground and I find myself the only one left staring at what the pieces of my colleagues I couldn't use.
