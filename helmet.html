Day Fifteen, Helmet

Go Back

Known as the Helmet for it's unique and distinctive design, he was feared and admired throughout the kingdom. Never seen not by the side of his princess, he was a mountain of a man, especially in contrast to his charge, towering over all who approached. No one but his princess ever heard him speak; he would issue most commands with a look, and expect his subordinates to carry them out without questions.

He would never socialise, never marry, No beer touched his lips, nor did the smoke of any tobacco fill his lungs. He was devout to his role, to the point of madness, though none of his movements or actions would give him away as such. It was only in those moments alone, when he would ready himself for sleep, and remove his steel and heavy helmet, that he would allow himself a moment to think about life. A man devoted to one cause, and never allowing himself a moment's hestitation, he would reflect on the possibilities he may have missed.

You only get to to live your life once, and he had made his choice. Before laying down in his bed his resolution would harden and his determination to see things through would be complete. He'd sleep a dreamless sleep, impatient to begin the next day.
