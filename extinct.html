Go Back

Ah, the long lost dodo. They say the last was seen in the mid-17th century, and their extinction caused quite the commotion among humanity with regards to our involvement on the animal kingdom and our effect on their ecosystems.

Our army is numerous and highly trained in the art of skewering humans and roasting them above a spit for food. We will be top of the food chain soon. Attacks will be launched simultaneously across the African continent and from there we will hunt down every human across the globe.

The only way to survive on this planet is to make them what they thought they had made us.. extinct.
