Day Nineteen, Loop

Go Back

He was determined to be the best. The champion. The most daring, brave and sexually desired skeleton in all the world. He'd have his boney profile in every magazine around the world and everybody would be talking about him around the graveyard.

He revved his engine with excitement as he looked ahead of him at the task at hand. The tarmac hot and glistening in the summer heat leading to the enormous loop in front of him, heaving upwards in a way that was disorienting. The entire road twirled in on itself and was held together by huge metal struts on all sides, like giant scaffolding. It had been tested thoroughly, and as long as he reached the required speed at the crucial point, he'd make it.

He revved some more and felt the moment coming nearer and nearer. He put an arm out of his window like a composer letting his crowd know the first note of his magnum opus was about to be played. The crowd roared with excitement and not a small amount of bonelust and this was all the starting gun he needed to put his foot and speed towards the loop that would propel him to fame and fortune.
