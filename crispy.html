Day Twenty-Eight, Crispy

Go Back

Chicken is fucking good isn't it? I mean really good. But for me chicken was an addiction that went out of hand. I fell for chicken and then I fell for the spice, and then I fell for the fire.

I wanted it crispier, spicier; I wanted more and more. And now here I am offering blackened crispy chicken with the fire of a thousand suns to all the people on Samhain. Seemed like the right time to do so, and I try to stay in doors the rest of the year on account of all my skin having charred off from my addictive habit.

Gimme a call sometime.
