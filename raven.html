Day Five, Raven

Go Back

You know about werewolves right? Regular people most of the time, until the full moon comes along and they turn into wolves, limb by excruciating limb, like in those movies. Well, the Daughters of the Raven worship a deity who had similar power, but instead of turning into a wolf it turned into a huge raven.

Other than that, same deal, normal lady rest of the time. She'd go around her worshipping communities gifting good luck, or bountiful crops or whatever else as she went. Then the full moon would come along and the transformation would occur. I imagine it was a bit less visceral than a werewolf transformation; almost beautiful, with feathers all over the place and beams of light and dark just like in the images.

In her raven form, the goddess is meant to not deal out good luck, but take revenge on those who don't believe or who do any harm to her worshippers. From giver to defender.. but I don't believe in any of that stuff. Do you?
