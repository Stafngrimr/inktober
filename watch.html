Day Eight, Watch

Go Back

He watches over the town as it gets ready for bed. Each person so small from atop his tower, finishing off their day and getting ready to cosy up near loved ones and sleep.

But he's not really that interested in those whose day is going to end peacefully. He made these goggles to be able to see the small details, the fear in people's eyes, the bad men skulking in the shadows. He is waiting for someone to misbehave, so he can rush to their rescue. He's been up in this tower ever since he was small dreaming of being a hero, dreaming of the day he could prove himself to all below as their saviour.

But the town doesn't go through that many crises. It's a quiet, sleepy town, filled with people who cherish and respect each other. So he watches, and he waits.
