Day Twenty-Seven, Spark

Go Back

Life is actually very complicated. It works on many different planets across many different universes and the most basic building blocks are the same, but the proportion of each block and then the ingredients on top make huge differences as to the survival of one species against another.

Then you have to worry about the ecosystem of different species working together and making sure that they balane each other nicely. The right amount of predators and prey, the right amount of flora and fauna, the right amount of everything in between. Some planets just manage this by luck, by the thousand monkeys typing out millions of millenia of rubbish to produce the masterpiece of life.

Some planets need a spark. A spark of hope, a spark of love, a spark of intelligence to put the pieces together in the right place and make sure that life does find a way, whether it likes it or not. Does that make me a creator?
