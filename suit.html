5th April 1885 - The London apartment of Prof. Dick R. Robertson was broken into over the weekend. Against the Professor's wishes one of his kindly neighbours alerted the authorities, which lead to a more grave discovery than that of a minor larceny.

When questioning the Professor and searching the apartment for clues, the constabulary found evidence of a secret laboratory into which they decided to check on account of the Professor's suspicious behaviour. A creature was found, appearing to be from the sea, wired into a suit made from metal plates and wires. The police inmediately blocked all access to the area but an image has surfaced, which you find attached to the article.

The poor creature appears to be a cephalopod coerced into a state of hypnosis, through the use of modern technology. The globe upon it's head is filled with some kind of dark matter, projecting an image of space into it's mind. Reports say the creature moved upright within the suit, but moved slowly and without appearing to be aware of it's actual surroundings..
